QSanguosha ANIME MOD

An Open Source MOD of QSanguosha

QSanguosha Anime MOD is an open source project on Github following GPLv3 License. Other additional sources and materials follows CC BY-NC-ND 4.0 License.2

The game thread follows the rule of Sanguosha Standard Mode.

There are 6 camps in this game: Science, Magic, Real, Touhou, Kancolle and Diva.

Science

Characters from science fictions or scientific structed world

Magic

Characters from fantasy stories or magic based world

Real

Characters from the stories in real world

Touhou

Characters from Touhou Project

Kancolle

Characters from Kancolle and other "Fleet Girl" games

Diva

Divas or idols from musical fictions or related stories
